My Childhood

My childhood history is full of very happy memories
and also very sad ones. I enjoyed my young age life with my mother Mary, DAD Kamau, my brother John and my sister Agnes.
I come from a humble background
where we used to survive for livelyhood.
We used to walk over along distance to look for water and firewood.
Me and my siblings we used to start our journey of 10km in the morning to look for firewood.
We were supporsed to be back before 12 o'oclock.
Later in the day after lunch my brother was to take our cattles to the feild to graze.
Me,my sister and mum we would go to fetch water in the river that was 5km away from home.
That was for the days that we were at home for school holidays.
School days parents could try and manage themselves to give us ammple time to go to school.
This helped me to be hardworking and eager to learn nd change someone's life.

Family Interactions

During holidays my cousins used to come to our home we would spend good time with them.
Especially during rain season we would run on the rain,
slide on mad and trap small insects which as a result of rain.
We would cook the insects behind our parent's back and eat them with ugali.
